Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

StreakWar ("we", "our", or "us") is a fitness streak and challenge app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address and password (for email sign-up)
  • Name and username chosen by you
  • Profile photo (optional)

Health and fitness data

With your explicit permission, StreakWar may read the following data from Android Health Connect or Apple HealthKit:

  • Step count
  • Exercise sessions (type, duration, distance, calories burned)
  • Active calories burned

This data is used solely to track your streaks, award challenge points, and display your activity history inside the app. We do not sell or share your health data with third parties.

Workout posts

  • Workouts you log manually, including activity type, duration, distance, steps, and any caption or photo you add

Connected third-party apps

If you connect Strava, StreakWar receives your activity data from that provider to sync workouts automatically. We store only the data needed to display your activities in the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate and maintain your activity streaks
  • Award points and rank participants in fitness challenges
  • Display your activity feed to other members of your challenges
  • Send streak reminder notifications (you can disable these at any time)
  • Improve the app and fix bugs

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in these limited cases:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider, used to store your account and workout data securely.
  • RevenueCat — used to manage Pro subscriptions. RevenueCat does not receive your health data.
  • Other challenge members — your username, streak count, and workout posts are visible to other members of challenges you join.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

4. Health Connect Data

StreakWar's use of health data obtained through Android Health Connect is limited to the features described in this policy. We do not use Health Connect data for advertising purposes. Health data is not transferred to third parties except as described above. You can revoke Health Connect permissions at any time through the Health Connect app on your device.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us at the address below, which will result in the permanent deletion of your personal data and workout history.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for health data access at any time via your device settings

7. Children

StreakWar is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email.
