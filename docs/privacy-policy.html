StreakWar Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Introduction

StreakWar ("we", "our", or "us") operates the StreakWar mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email address, and username when you register
  • Profile information you choose to provide

Health and Fitness Data

  • Workout activity data (type, duration, distance, calories) from Apple Health, Google Health Connect, or Strava
  • Step count data from your device or connected apps
  • We only read health data — we never write to Apple Health or Health Connect

Device and Usage Data

  • Push notification tokens to send you updates
  • Device platform (iOS or Android)

Third-Party Service Tokens

  • OAuth access tokens for Strava (stored encrypted, used only to fetch your workouts)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the StreakWar service
  • To track your workout streaks and award points in challenges
  • To send you push notifications about challenges and weekly recaps
  • To display your progress on leaderboards within challenges you join

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider
  • Expo / Apple / Google — for push notification delivery
  • Strava — when you connect your Strava account
  • RevenueCat — for processing in-app subscriptions

Other users in the same challenge can see your workout activity, points, and ranking within that challenge.

5. Health Data

Health data (workouts, steps) is used only to calculate points and streaks within the app. It is stored in our secure database and is not shared with third parties beyond what is listed above. You can disconnect health integrations at any time from the app settings.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us, which will result in permanent deletion of your data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Disconnect health and fitness integrations at any time

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (TLS), encrypted storage of OAuth tokens, and row-level security on our database. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

StreakWar is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.
